Overview

Kubernetes is arguably the most important container management technology in the world. This Seminar will take you from the basics of K8S objects to the depths of utilizing K8S as your multi-tier microservices application backbone, from theory to best practices, planning a microservices architecture, migrating from Monolith This workshop gives you the opportunity to put your hands on K8S and perform basic administration on it.
